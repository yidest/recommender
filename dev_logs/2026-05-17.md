## Session 7 Experiment

Command:

```bash
python -m src.experiments --n-components 5 10 20 50 --top-k 100 --max-users 100 --output /private/tmp/svd_components_decision.csv
```

Results:

| n_components | RMSE | HitRate@100 | Precision@100 | elapsed_seconds |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5 | 0.9842 | 0.0800 | 0.0008 | 139.4 |
| 10 | 0.9840 | 0.0700 | 0.0007 | 143.0 |
| 20 | 0.9839 | 0.0700 | 0.0007 | 144.2 |
| 50 | 0.9829 | 0.0800 | 0.0008 | 146.3 |

Decision:

- Updated `DEFAULT_N_COMPONENTS` from `20` to `50`.
- Reason: `50` had the best RMSE and tied for best `HitRate@100`, with only a small training-time increase over `20`.
